You are a Spy!

1. Your mission is to identify and kill the other spies on the station.
To assist in this task, you may collect wanted items around the station and ship them off in exchange for weapons.

2. To unlock a PDA:
1. Put your PDA in an empty hand and click on it.
2. Under 'General Functions', select 'Messenger'.
3. Click 'Set Ring Message'.
4. Enter the password.

3. Try to collect bounties before the other spies do. Once you collect enough bounties, the enemy spies' identities will be revealed to you. Use stealth. Keep your identity as a spy hidden from other players and security.
