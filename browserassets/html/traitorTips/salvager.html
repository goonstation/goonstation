You are a pirate salvager!

1. Use your salvage reclaimer to disassemble walls and doors.

2. Use your omnitool and deconstructor to convert equipment to frames for easy transportation.

3. Sell your spoils to the M4GP13 Salvage and Barter System for additional gear.

4. The Salvager Pods are specially equipped to return back to the Magpie when using the "Return to Station" function.

5. Your objectives will always be stored in your notes. To access them, use the Notes verb.
