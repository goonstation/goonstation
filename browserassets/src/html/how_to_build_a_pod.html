Making a Space Pod!

Be advised that you have to stand adjacent to the south west corner of the pod in order to construct it, since it is a 64x64 sprite. Use a bottle of champagne on the finished pod to give it a unique name.

You will need:

  • A preassembled pod kit
  • A set of pod armour
  • (Optional: a paint job kit)
  • A screwdriver
  • A wrench
  • A welder

To make:

  1. Deploy the frame kit by clicking on the box.
  2. Wrench the jumble of metal. It should look less like a pile of junk.
  3. Weld it. It should look like a functional but unsecured frame of a space pod.
  4. Use the screwdriver.
  5. Put armor on the pod frame.
  6. Wrench the now armored frame
  7. Use the welder.
  8. Use a screwdriver to close up the final parts
  9. Optionally, use the paint job kit on the finished pod.
