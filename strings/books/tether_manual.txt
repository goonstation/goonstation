<h1>Gravi-Tonne™ Tether Operator's Manual</h1>
<p><i>The Terra Nivium Gravi-Tonne™ Wide-area Gravity Tether brings gravity to you! This manual will show you how to operate and maintain the Gravi-Tonne™ Tether safely.</i></p>
<hr>
<h3>CHANGING GRAVITY</h3>
<p>To change gravity, follow these easy steps:
<ol>
	<li>Unlock the machine with an <b>authorized ID</b>.</li>
	<li>Enter the desired gravity intensity <b>by hand</b>.</li>
	<li>Relax while the Gravi-Tonne™ Tether <b>processes</b> the change.</li>
	<li>Be sure to re-secure the tether after use!</b>.</li>
</ol>
<p><i>Did you know..? 1 G-Force is equal to Earth's gravity!</i></p>

<h3>STARTING MAINTENANCE</h3>
<p>The Gravi-Tonne™ Tether has a front access door that holds the internal power capacitor. The tamper-resist security grate is withdrawn when the system is unlocked.</p>
<p>In case of emergency, the tamper-resist grate can be cut through with a rotary saw. Verify internal wiring is not damaged after removing the tamper-resist grate.</p>

<h4>INTERNAL CELL REPLACEMENT</h4>
<p>To replace the internal capacitor, follow these easy steps:</p>
<ol>
	<li>Unlock the machine with an <b>authorized ID</b>.</li>
	<li>Carefully open the maintenance panel by <b>prying</b> it open.</li>
	<li>Loosen the internal capacitor cell's bolts with a <b>wrench</b></li>
	<li>Only replace with official TerraNivium Gravi-Tonne™ Gravi-Cells™</li>
</ol>
<p><i>Did you know..? Unstable batteries will damage or destroy your Gravi-Tonne™ Tether.</i></p>

<h4>SYSTEM REPAIR/RESET</h4>
<p>To perform a full repair and reset of the Gravi-Tonne™ Tether, follow these easy steps:
<ol>
<li>Remove the internal capacitor cell as described above.</li>
<li>Once grounded, carefully remove damaged wiring by <b>cutting</b> them out.</li>
<li>Replace the removed wiring with fresh <b>cables</b>.</li>
<li>After the system restarts, set the desired gravity intensity.</li>
</ol>

<p><i>Did you know..? Lack of proper wire maintenance is the leading cause of tether malfunctions!</i></p>

<h3>INDICATOR LIGHTS</h3>
<p>The Gravi-Tonne™ Tether uses multiple visual indicators for quick verification of system operation.</p>
<ul>
	<li><b>Charge</b>: Top-right LEDs<ul>
		<li><b>Blue</b>: Full</li>
		<li><b>Green</b>: Mostly Full</li>
		<li><b>Yellow</b>: Under Half</li>
		<li><b>Red</b>: Critical</li>
		<li><b>Flashing</b>: Missing Battery</li>
	</ul></li>
	<li><b>Status</b>: Top-left LEDs<ul>
		<li><b>Green / Steady</b>: Online, Active</li>
		<li><b>Yellow / Blinking</b>: Online, Idle</li>
		<li><b>Red / Random</b>: Error/Fault</li>
		<li><b>Blue / Bouncing</b>: Processing</li>
	</ul></li>
	<li><b>Intensity</b>: Outer Left Edge<ul>
		<li><b>Red</b>: Extreme Gravity</li>
		<li><b>Yellow</b>: High Gravity</li>
		<li><b>Green</b>: Earth-like Gravity</li>
		<li><b>Blue Steady</b>: Microgravity</li>
		<li><b>Blue Flashing</b>: Zero-G
	</ul></li>
	<li><b>Detector</b>: Above Maintenance Door<ul>
		<li><b>Blank</b>: No Power</li>
		<li><b>Green</b>: Nominal Readings</li>
		<li><b>Yellow</b>: Low / No Gravity</li>
		<li><b>Red</b>: Active Gravity Crisis</li>
	</ul></li>
	<li><b>Input</b>: Middle-right Screen<ul>
		<li><b>Green</b>: Unlocked</li>
		<li><b>Yellow</b>: Locked</li>
		<li><b>Red</b>: Error</li>
		<li><b>Blue</b>: Calibrating for next use</li>
	</ul></li>
	<li><b>Dials</b>: Right outer edge<ul>
		<li><b>Clockwise</b>: Increasing Intensity</li>
		<li><b>Anti-Clockwise</b>: Decreasing Intensity</li>
		<li><b>Slight Variance</b>: Nominal Operation</li>
	</ul></li>
</ul>
